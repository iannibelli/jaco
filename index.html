Welcome to IAnnibelli’s blog! Here we share ideas, discoveries, projects, and reflections on digital culture, connected society, and human technologies.
I am a student in the field of digital humanities. My work explores how technology affects society, knowledge, and ethics.
Recently, I have been writing responses to Anatomy of an AI System's Article and The Internet’s Own Boy Documentary, among other important themes. These projects help me to think, analise and reflect about the social and human sides of digital systems, from artificial intelligence to online activism. I am also interested in questions about access to information, such as whether all archives should be open to everyone. Through my studies, I hope to understand how digital technologies can both connect and challenge us in today’s world.
